Welcome

Welcome to the Oshikawa Group at the Institute for Solid State Physics (ISSP), University of Tokyo. Our group focuses on theoretical condensed matter physics, with particular interests in quantum many-body systems, topological phases of matter, and low-dimensional quantum systems.

Research

Our research focuses on fundamental problems in theoretical condensed matter physics:

  • Quantum Many-Body Systems: Investigation of strongly correlated electron systems and quantum phase transitions
  • Topological Phases: Study of topological order, topological insulators, and topological quantum computation
  • Low-Dimensional Systems: Analysis of one-dimensional and two-dimensional quantum systems
  • Conformal Field Theory: Applications to condensed matter physics
