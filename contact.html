Directions

The Institute for Solid State Physics is located in Kashiwa Campus of the University of Tokyo.

By Train:
From Tokyo Station: Take the JR Joban Line to Kashiwa Station (approximately 40 minutes). From Kashiwa Station, take a bus or taxi to the campus (approximately 15 minutes).

By Bus:
Buses are available from Kashiwa Station (East Exit) to the University of Tokyo Kashiwa Campus.

For detailed directions and campus maps, please visit the ISSP Access Page.

Visitors

If you plan to visit our group, please contact us in advance to arrange a meeting. Visitors to the campus may need to register at the entrance.
